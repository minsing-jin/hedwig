Five Moats That Make Hedwig Different

🎙️

Socratic Onboarding

An LLM interviewer asks you questions until your criteria are crystal clear. No manual config files. No settings menus. Just a conversation.

🧬

Self-Evolving Algorithm

Inspired by Karpathy's autoresearch pattern. Daily micro-mutations + weekly macro-evolution. The system experiments with itself and keeps improvements.

👍

Boolean-Only Feedback

Just upvote or downvote. The system handles pattern interpretation. No rating scales, no tagging, no effort. Your time matters.

🧠

Long-Horizon Memory

Weekly preference snapshots track your taste trajectory over months. Hedwig understands you better than yesterday, better than last week.

👑

Algorithm Sovereignty

Unlike YouTube or X — optimized for ad revenue — Hedwig optimizes for your definition of relevance. You control the fitness function.

😈

Devil's Advocate

Every signal includes a counter-perspective. Hedwig doesn't just tell you what to pay attention to — it tells you what to doubt.
