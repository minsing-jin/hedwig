Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-04-11

What We Collect

  • Account info: email, OAuth provider ID, optional display name
  • Criteria: the filtering preferences you create or that are inferred from your inputs
  • Feedback: upvote/downvote signals and optional natural-language feedback
  • SNS handles: handles you provide for auto-context inference (we fetch public profile data only)
  • Usage metrics: LLM tokens, signals collected — for quota enforcement

What We DO NOT Collect

  • Your private DMs or messages on any platform
  • Your location
  • Your browsing history outside Hedwig
  • Cookies for advertising

How We Use Your Data

  • To personalize your signal feed
  • To improve your criteria via the evolution engine (your data only — never aggregated with others)
  • To enforce subscription quotas
  • To send transactional emails (account, billing)

Data Isolation

All user data is stored in Supabase with Row-Level Security (RLS) policies enforcing per-user isolation. No user can read another user's signals, criteria, or feedback.

Third Parties

  • Supabase: Database + Auth (data stored in their secure infrastructure)
  • OpenAI: LLM scoring (your criteria sent in inference calls; OpenAI does not train on API data per their policy)
  • Stripe: Payment processing (no payment info stored on our servers)
  • r.jina.ai: Content normalization (URLs only, no auth)

Your Rights

  • Export: Download all your data at /settings/export
  • Delete: Delete your account and all data at /settings/delete
  • Correct: Update your criteria, profile, settings anytime
  • GDPR/CCPA: EU and California residents have additional rights — contact us

Cookies

We use only essential cookies: session token (httponly) for authentication. No tracking, no advertising.

Contact

Privacy questions: privacy@hedwig.app
